# How to Navigate the Grocery App

This app is a **Platform** that connects Store Owners with Customers.

## Flow 1: For Store Owners 
**Where to start?**
1. Go to existing store: `http://localhost:5174/store/shop-a/admin`
2. **Or Create New Store**: Click on **"+ Create Your Store (Demo)"** at the bottom of the home page.

**What to do?**
1. **Setup**: Go to *Store Settings* tab. Change your Store Name and Theme Color.
2. **Stock**: Go to *Products* tab. Add new items you want to sell.
3. **Manage**: Go to *Orders* tab. Wait for customers to order, then Verify and Deliver.

## Flow 2: For Customers
**Where to start?**
1. Go to the **Main Directory**: `http://localhost:5174/`
2. Choose a shop (e.g., "Ram's Kirana").

**What to do?**
1. **Browse**: Search for products (e.g., "Rice").
2. **Cart**: Add items to your bucket.
3. **Order**: Go to Checkout and click "Place Order".
4. **Wait**: The store owner will contact you on Viber/WhatsApp to confirm.
